About

The Food and Wine festival is held every year by Disney. It is one of the biggest food festivals in the world! The festival is held in California Adventure in L.A, California and Epcot in Orlando, Florida!. Many people come to the festival to try foods from around the world! Many famous chefs come to the festival to show their country's cuisine. There are many festival highlights, such as meeting famous chefs and tv personalities.

The chef shown here is a famous chef from the tv show "Cake Boss."

Cuisine

The cuisine at the festival is global and many countries' foods are represented within the festival. Many of the foods there are special because they are one of a kind! You won't be able to get many of the dishes anywhere else. There were many favorites and some of those favorites may stay in the festival's menu! Sopme of the favorites from last year were the Dry Rose Cider, Teriyaki SPAM Hash, Mango Bubble Tea, and more!
